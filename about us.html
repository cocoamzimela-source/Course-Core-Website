About Course Core

Course Core was founded in 2023 by Thandiswa Cocoa Mzimela after completing her Bachelor in Computer Science in South Africa. She recognized a gap in IT education where students received a lot of theory and some practical work, but not enough to truly prepare them for real jobs in IT companies.

Today, Course Core is run by Thandiswa, her team of front-end and back-end developers, and experienced lecturers who are passionate about sharing their knowledge. Our purpose is to help both beginners and professionals apply their skills practically, empowering developers to grow and succeed in real IT environments.

Our Mission

At Course Core, our mission is to close the gap between theory and practice in IT education. We ensure developers don’t just learn concepts but actually use them in real projects and workplaces.


Our Vision

We envision a world where every developer has the opportunity to grow, succeed, and contribute meaningfully in the tech industry. Course Core aims to be a community that inspires learning, empowers growth, and prepares developers for the future.

Why Choose Us?

  • Personalized Experience: Weekly student feedback helps us adapt and maintain a high standard of education.
  • Interactive Learning: Our online classes are not one-sided. Lecturers engage directly with students and are invested in their success.
  • Affordable Education: We keep our courses accessible, ensuring that learners from all backgrounds can benefit.
